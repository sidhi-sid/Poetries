PILLOWS



Remember,those pillow forts
Where we used to gather
All the pillows available
And the fights for
Not letting someone in
Your fragile yet satisfying fort
Pillows made us feel like architects, no

And when during a fight
We would throw one
At their faces
As a greatest weapon
We could afford
Pillows saved us, no

At times,helped in sleeping comfortably
With the bruised knees,
Aching backs, joints
Pillows have been supportive ,no

And when we needed to hug
A companion
At oddest hours to ease
Our bodies and our souls
Pillows have been available,no

And whenever we slept on our stomach
To weep
Or to feel more of a pal
And less of ourselves
Pillows occupied us, no

In this variable world , pillows remained constant with our souls,no.
